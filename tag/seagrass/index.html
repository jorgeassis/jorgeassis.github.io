Seagrass meadows

Predicting climate change impacts on seagrasses to aid conservation

Our work on seagrass meadows focuses on developing predictive models and analytical tools to understand climate change impacts on these vital ecosystems. This research helps inform conservation strategies and promote their resilience in a rapidly changing world.
